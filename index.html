Executive Profile

Thea Olivier is a FinTech operations executive, thought leader and customer advocate serving as Executive MCA SA Operations. Her work sits at the critical nexus of financial technology infrastructure and real-world human impact.

Her career spans high-growth South African digital environments, from scaled commerce operations to alternative lending and digital banking integration. She is recognised as an operations leader who treats friction reduction, speed and accessibility as customer advocacy.

Her strategic focus includes gender-intelligent customer value propositions, expanding funding access for underrepresented entrepreneurs, and operational models that leverage alternative transaction data to reduce structural barriers and create more inclusive financial ecosystems.
